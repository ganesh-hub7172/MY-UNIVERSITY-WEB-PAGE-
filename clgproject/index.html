Degree

An academic degree is a qualification awarded to a student upon successful completion of a course of study in higher education. Academic degrees are commonly structured in levels. In the UK and countries whose educational systems are based on the British system, honours degrees are divided into classes: first, second (broken into upper second, or 2.1, and lower second, or 2.2) and third class.

Post Graduation

Postgraduate study, or graduate school, involves advanced academic programs (Master's, Doctorate) and professional qualifications pursued after a Bachelor's degree, focusing on specialized knowledge, research, or professional skills through taught courses or dissertations, common in UK/Commonwealth (postgraduate) and US (graduate) systems, offering certificates, diplomas, or degrees like MA, MSc, MBA, or PhD.
